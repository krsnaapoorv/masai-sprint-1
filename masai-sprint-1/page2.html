Equipments

Racquets

Badminton racquets are lightweight, with top quality racquets weighing between 70 and 95 grams.They are made up of carbon fiber,steel of different shape and size.


Strings/Net

Badminton strings for racquets are thin, high performing strings with thicknesses ranging from about 0.62 to 0.73 mm.Thicker strings are more durable, but many players prefer the feel of thinner strings. String tension is normally in the range of 80 to 160 N.

Shuttlecock

A shuttlecock/shuttle,also called a birdie,is a high-drag projectile, with an open conical shape: the cone is formed from sixteen overlapping feathers embedded into a rounded cork base. The cork is covered with thin leather or synthetic material.

Nets

Badminton nets should measure 5-feet high from the center of the court and 5-feet, 1-inch high where the net attaches to the poles. The depth of the net should be 2-feet, 6-inches and the thickness of the net should be somewhere between 0.5 inches and 0.8 inches.
